About Me

Who's this guy anyway?

I’m a Web Developer based in Panamá City. I have serious passion in creating, designing & developing winning websites.

A bit about my story

Initially my journey in the tech field started at the end of 2019 when I took HTML && CSS lessons at FreeCode Academy, however it wasn't until 2021 that I made the decision to delve deeper into this field. I started studying Python but it didn't seem that interesting at first. I did a bit more research on websites and immediately started taking HTML and CSS classes and finally added Javascript.

By the way I took Google's UI/UX course and noticed that I really enjoy creating, and making my projects look more beautiful and more user-friendly. After a couple of months studying Bootstrap, Node.js, React and SQL; I took up Python classes to deploy my own web applications and put my Front-End Developments together.

I am currently still looking for opportunities to advance my career as a Web Developer and have as main focus to use my skills to contribute to large projects.

Outside of this, I like to spend time racing rental karts, playing the Guitar with my Rock band, whether reading books or speaking about philosophy with my friends.
